Treemap Prog. Summative

by awphi

Here is an example of the Treemap class in action at it's most basic level. It's a visual representation of the land area of all countries in the UN as of 2005. It demonstrates the text clipping capabilities of the treemap (which had to be heavily modified from functions that were available on Observable but not in vanilla JS/jQuery.

This is an example of a Treemap which shows off the 'dynamic reloading' which I implemented. The dataset can be reloaded on the fly and the treemap will be rebuilt and refreshed ASAP. This feature was not available in the original but after converting it into a class it became apparent the getters/setters were redundant without dynamically updating the treemap in the DOM.

d3 class treemap modified from the original by Mike Bostock, creator of d3.

Formatting & other utilities from Bootstrap and jQuery.

Treemap data from the UN's 'Land' dataset from 2005, 2010, 2016 & 2018 - provided by Awesome Public Datasets.
